What is Pour Over?

Pour over is manual brewing. Every variable is exposed, which means every mistake is visible — and every improvement is earned.

Bloom

Fresh coffee releases trapped CO₂ when water hits it. Blooming lets gases escape so extraction can happen evenly.

Pour

Controlled pouring maintains an even slurry. Circular motion prevents channeling.

Drawdown

Final extraction occurs as water drains. Time here reveals grind and ratio errors.

Control Variables

  • Grind Size
  • Water Temperature
  • Coffee Ratio
  • Pour Speed

Common Failures

Bitter coffee is often over-extraction. Sour coffee usually means under-extraction. The fix is rarely changing everything at once.

How to Practice

Change one variable at a time. Taste. Adjust. Repeat.
